Instructions

  • Click on the canvas to add a point.

  • Click on the "Show Unsolved" checkbox to show the unsolved solution.
  • Click on the "Color by Distance" checkbox to color the lines by distance.
  • Slide the "Speed" slider to change the animation speed.
